Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond or coconut milk)
  • Optional toppings: shaved chocolate, berries, nuts

Procedure:

  1. Peel and pit the avocados, then place them in a blender or food processor.
  2. Add cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla extract, salt, and non-dairy milk to the blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.
  4. Taste and adjust sweetness if necessary by adding more maple syrup.
  5. Transfer the mousse to serving bowls or glasses.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to chill and set.
  7. Before serving, garnish with shaved chocolate, berries, or nuts if desired.
  8. Enjoy this indulgent and healthy vegan dessert!
