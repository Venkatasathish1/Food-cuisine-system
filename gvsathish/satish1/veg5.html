Ingredients:

  • 8 oz (225g) pasta
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, peas)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil and butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  5. Add cooked pasta to the pan and toss to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley on top.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve hot.
