Ingredients:

  • 8 oz (225g) rice noodles
  • 1 cup tofu, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped peanuts
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce

Procedure:

  1. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and cubed tofu. Saut√© until tofu is golden brown.
  4. Add sliced bell pepper, bean sprouts, and sliced green onions. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, lime juice, maple syrup, rice vinegar, and sriracha sauce.
  6. Add cooked rice noodles and sauce to the pan. Toss to combine and heat through.
  7. Divide into serving bowls and garnish with chopped peanuts and cilantro.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
