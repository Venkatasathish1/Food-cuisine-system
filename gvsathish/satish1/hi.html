Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cups water

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add thinly sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  5. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Mix well.
  6. Add chicken pieces and yogurt. Cook until chicken is partially cooked.
  7. Meanwhile, boil water in a separate pot and add soaked rice. Cook rice until it's 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  8. Once chicken is partially cooked, add chopped mint leaves and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  9. In a large pot, layer half of the cooked rice, followed by the cooked chicken mixture. Repeat the layers.
  10. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and the rice is tender.
  11. Once done, gently fluff the biryani with a fork and serve hot with raita or salan.

Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, garam masala, and salt. Mix well to make the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and coat them evenly. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight, to marinate.
  3. Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.
  5. Brush the skewered chicken with vegetable oil.
  6. Grill the chicken skewers for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and slightly charred on the outside.
  7. If using an oven, preheat it to 200°C (400°F) and bake the skewers for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  8. Once cooked, remove the chicken tikka from the grill or oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  9. Serve hot with sliced onions, lemon wedges, and mint chutney on the side.

Fish Bites

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams fish fillets (any white fish like cod or tilapia)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Lemon wedges for serving
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Procedure:

  1. In a shallow dish, mix together flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. In another shallow dish, whisk together beaten egg and milk.
  3. Pat fish fillets dry with paper towels and cut into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Dip each fish piece into the flour mixture, then into the egg mixture, and back into the flour mixture.
  6. Shake off excess flour and carefully place fish pieces into the hot oil.
  7. Fry fish for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove fish from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  9. Serve hot with lemon wedges and garnish with fresh parsley.

Grilled Salmon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush salmon fillets with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place lemon slices on top of each fillet.
  4. Grill salmon for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  5. Remove from grill and garnish with fresh dill.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite side dishes.

Spicy Garlic Shrimp Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
+

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, or until pink and cooked through.
  5. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley.
  6. Serve hot as an appetizer or with pasta/rice.

Mutton Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg mutton, cut into pieces
  • 3 onions, finely sliced
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cloves
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Water as needed

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a pressure cooker or large pot.
  2. Add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom pods. Sauté for a minute.
  3. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  6. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add mutton pieces and yogurt. Cook for 5-6 minutes.
  8. Add water as needed to cover the mutton pieces. Close the pressure cooker lid or cover the pot with a lid.
  9. Cook for 4-5 whistles in a pressure cooker or simmer for 1.5-2 hours on low heat until the mutton is tender.
  10. Once mutton is cooked, garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  11. Serve hot with rice or roti.

Mutton Korma

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams mutton, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cloves
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts, soaked in water
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat ghee or vegetable oil in a pan.
  2. Add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom pods. Sauté for a minute.
  3. Add finely sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  6. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add mutton pieces and yogurt. Cook for 5-6 minutes.
  8. In a blender, blend soaked cashew nuts with a little water to make a smooth paste.
  9. Add the cashew nut paste to the pan and mix well.
  10. Cover and cook on low heat until the mutton is tender, stirring occasionally.
  11. Once mutton is cooked, add fresh cream and mix well.
  12. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
