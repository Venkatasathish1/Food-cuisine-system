Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush salmon fillets with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place lemon slices on top of each fillet.
  4. Grill salmon for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  5. Remove from grill and garnish with fresh dill.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite side dishes.
