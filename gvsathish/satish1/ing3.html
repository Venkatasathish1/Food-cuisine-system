Ingredients:

  • 500 grams fish fillets (any white fish like cod or tilapia)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Lemon wedges for serving
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Procedure:

  1. In a shallow dish, mix together flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. In another shallow dish, whisk together beaten egg and milk.
  3. Pat fish fillets dry with paper towels and cut into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Dip each fish piece into the flour mixture, then into the egg mixture, and back into the flour mixture.
  6. Shake off excess flour and carefully place fish pieces into the hot oil.
  7. Fry fish for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove fish from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  9. Serve hot with lemon wedges and garnish with fresh parsley.
