Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Add shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, or until pink and cooked through.
  5. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley.
  6. Serve hot as an appetizer or with pasta/rice.
