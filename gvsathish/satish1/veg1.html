Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, potatoes), diced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Heat ghee or vegetable oil in a pan.
  2. Add cumin seeds, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom pods. Sauté for a minute.
  3. Add thinly sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  6. Add diced vegetables, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Drain soaked rice and add it to the pan. Stir well to coat the rice with the vegetables.
  8. Add water and bring to a boil.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  10. Once done, fluff the pulao with a fork and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  11. Serve hot with raita or salad.
