Ingredients:

  • 1 kg mutton, cut into pieces
  • 3 onions, finely sliced
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cloves
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Water as needed

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a pressure cooker or large pot.
  2. Add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom pods. Sauté for a minute.
  3. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  6. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add mutton pieces and yogurt. Cook for 5-6 minutes.
  8. Add water as needed to cover the mutton pieces. Close the pressure cooker lid or cover the pot with a lid.
  9. Cook for 4-5 whistles in a pressure cooker or simmer for 1.5-2 hours on low heat until the mutton is tender.
  10. Once mutton is cooked, garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  11. Serve hot with rice or roti.
