Ingredients:

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Salsa and hot sauce for serving

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add drained black beans, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until heated through.
  3. Warm corn tortillas in a separate skillet or oven.
  4. Assemble tacos by filling each tortilla with black beans, sliced avocado, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, chopped red onions, and cilantro.
  5. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the tacos.
  6. Serve hot with salsa, hot sauce, and additional lime wedges on the side.
  7. Enjoy these delicious vegan tacos!
