Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, onions, olives, mushrooms)
  • 1/4 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the pre-made pizza crust on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Arrange mixed vegetables, cherry tomatoes, black olives, red onions, and mushrooms on top of the cheese.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the vegetables.
  7. Season with dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve hot.
